GRNET's Authentication and Authorization Infrastructure (AAI) assists intra-organizational services by providing federated access control between GRNET's partner organizations. Using GRNET's AAI, the Institutions belonging to GRNET's federation may provide services to users of fellow federated Institutions in a transparent, safe and easy manner.

GRNET's federation is primarily targeted to the Hellenic Academic and Research community and anyone interested in providing services towards this community. The main aim is the co-ordination of the co-operation between the different institutions and the assistance of development of intra-institutional services.

The adoption of such services by the Identity Providers, i.e. the Institutions, yields multiple benefits:

  • logging into a service provided by a third party does not require a separate account, the user may use his/her current institutional account.
  • every service provider participating in the federation does not need to know the access credential of the users accessing their service. Service providers need only to trust the users' Home Organizations.
  • there is support for fully anonymous, trusted access and credentials including the user's identity, position and affiliation are only optionally transmitted to each service provider. In cases where the provided service relies on particular end-user attributes, the federation requires the Identity Provides to aprove the release of these attributes and the end-users are notified accordingly.

Accordingly, a Service Provider may benefit from joining the federation by utilizing a simple and powerful access control mechanism when providing services to the Hellenic Academic and Research community, either in part, or in whole.

The following services are currently provided over GRNET's AAI:

  • "Pithos" Data storage service
  • Microsoft DreamSpark, free access to software for students
  • ΑΝΑΦΑΝΔΟΝ-MSDNAA, free access to software for students through the MSDNAA initiative
  • IPTV service, digital television broadcasting
  • Teleconference service VoIP service (Real Time Services)

At the same time, access is provided to eduGAIN's european AAI services which was developed as part of GÈANT2, to ease co-operation between the academic community and European institutions.
