The use of the services provided over GRNET's AAI Federation requires an account in an institution that is member of the Federation.

Every Organization belonging to the Federation is required to safeguard the privacy of the credentials accompanying these accounts from unauthorized access.

The federated organizations providing user accounts disclose only the minimal required fragment of the account holders' credentials and only with the account holders' prior consent, acquired by means of a special informational web page presented to the account holder.

All services provided as part of the Federation do not require nor use personal credentials other than those required for the service's operation.

GRNET and GRNET's AAI Federation as a whole cannot be held responsible for the fulfilment of the above requirements on behalf of the Federation Members. GRNET and the Federation as a whole does not store nor transmit account data, it mereley describes safe ways of information exchange between the Federation's Members with the involved user's consent.

The Federation and its Members operate according to the laws concerning the protection of private data.
